Database - The visualization

Creating the database with google sheets for some micro business and stuff
and having the visualization for decision-making.

Sales with Data Studio

Goals

This project is designed for any micro business such as design consultants (for my case rn) with their database of sales and stuff. The database is made with VBA google sheet/AppsScript and Google Studio for the visualization.
So, our supervisor or boss can clearly see the progress of sales or whatever we choose to be. And we can have more accurate decision-making for their future company to thrive.
